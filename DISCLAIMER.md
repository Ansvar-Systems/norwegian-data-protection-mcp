## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

| Source | Authority Level | Verify At |
|--------|----------------|-----------|
| **Datatilsynet** | Authoritative for Norwegian data protection enforcement | [datatilsynet.no](https://www.datatilsynet.no/) |
| **Lovdata** | Authoritative for Norwegian legislation (personopplysningsloven) | [lovdata.no](https://lovdata.no/) |
| **Personvernnemnda** | Authoritative for appeals of Datatilsynet decisions | [personvernnemnda.no](https://www.personvernnemnda.no/) |

**This Tool contains summaries, not official legal text.** The official text published on datatilsynet.no and lovdata.no is the authoritative source.

## Currency and Staleness

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by weeks or months
- Norwegian data protection enforcement evolves through new decisions, EDPB guidance, and legislative amendments
- A decision marked "final" may have been appealed or superseded since the last database update
- This Tool does NOT automatically track appeals to Personvernnemnda

## Coverage Gaps

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Full legal text** -- contains representative summaries only
2. **Personvernnemnda decisions** -- Privacy Appeals Board rulings are not covered
3. **EU/EDPB guidelines** -- covered separately by the EU Regulations MCP
4. **Court judgments** -- Norwegian court decisions on data protection matters are not included
5. **Sector-specific guidance** -- guidance from other Norwegian regulators (Nasjonal kommunikasjonsmyndighet, Helsetilsynet, etc.) is not covered
6. **Historical decision versions** -- limited to current final versions
